Our Services

our strategy

Our goal is to provide world-class property advisory services to help people navigate the Tel Aviv real estate market. Thanks to our expertise both in Israel and internationally, we are able to bridge that gap. Above all, spending the time to know our clients lies at the core of our profession. We understand that home hunting is a highly personal and often nerve-wracking process. So we strive to make the process as smooth and enjoyable as possible.

our philosophy

is to only offer the most suitable options to our buyers after sifting through the entire range of properties available in the Tel Aviv real estate market. Only then do we curate. Furthermore, we understand that for each property, there is one specific buyer out there looking for it. We never lose sight that a property is above all a home.

our process

Similarly, when entrusted with the sale of a property, our method is analogous. No two properties are identical. First, our team provides a comprehensive real estate market analysis and professional valuation. We believe in numbers. This enables sellers to identify the future owner of their home.

Tel Aviv is an exciting place to live

In a few words, the Tel Avivi was founded by a group of like-minded international real estate professionals with a diverse background and, most importantly, a shared love for Tel Aviv, one of the world’s most exciting and dynamic cities (and it’s only just the beginning).

Equal parts blend of tech innovation, design, art, and both historic and modern architecture, Tel Aviv has become the ultimate Mediterranean metropolis and a popular flight destination for all. A buoyant foodie scene, sandy beaches, world-class museums, and an electric night-life are only a few of the attractions Tel Aviv has on offer.

Telavivians are fiercely passionate about their local cafe, and there are certainly hundreds to choose from, each one serving up a perfect cup of coffee. A third common characteristic of almost all of us Tel Avivim, is how resistant we are to moving out of the city. No matter how small and expensive our apartments are, and no matter how many parking tickets we get, we are by nature diehard Tel Avivim. Moving out to the suburbs is frowned upon and considered as a defection! People will go thru a lot just to stay in the city we love.

Ultimately, living in Tel Aviv is a lot of fun:
a blend of Barcelona and Brooklyn with a splash of Los Angeles and a generous serving of Casablanca!
